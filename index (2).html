Our Journey

CSK Legacy Timeline

A journey of resilience, glory, and unwavering spirit since 2008

2008

The Beginning

CSK was founded and acquired by India Cements for $91 million. MS Dhoni was signed as the marquee player for $1.5 million, becoming the face of the franchise.

Inception Dhoni Era Begins
2010

First IPL Title

CSK won their maiden IPL title by defeating Mumbai Indians by 22 runs in the final. Suresh Raina was the top scorer with 520 runs.

Champions IPL 2010
2010

CLT20 Champions

CSK became the first Indian team to win the Champions League T20, defeating Warriors by 8 wickets. Murali Vijay scored 58 in the final.

CLT20 Champs Double Crown
2011

Back-to-Back Champions

CSK defended their IPL title by beating Royal Challengers Bangalore by 58 runs. Murali Vijay's 95 and Ashwin's 3/16 were standout performances.

2nd IPL Title Dominance
2014

The Suspension

CSK faced a two-year suspension from IPL due to the spot-fixing scandal. The franchise was banned for the 2016 and 2017 seasons.

Suspended Dark Period
2018

The Grand Return

CSK returned after suspension and won their third IPL title, defeating Sunrisers Hyderabad by 8 wickets. Watson scored a magnificent 117*.

3rd IPL Title Comeback Kings
2021

4th IPL Title

CSK won their fourth IPL title by defeating Kolkata Knight Riders by 27 runs. Faf du Plessis scored 86 in the final at Dubai.

4th IPL Title Ageless
2023

5th IPL Title - The Record

CSK equaled MI's record with their 5th IPL title, defeating Gujarat Titans by 5 wickets (DLS method). Jadeja hit the winning runs.

5th IPL Title Record Equaled
2025

The Legacy Continues

CSK continues to build on their legendary status with a strong squad, nurturing young talent while maintaining their winning culture.
